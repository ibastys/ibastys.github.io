About me


My research interests revolve around information security and privacy, with a focus on language-based security techniques (such as information flow control). My goal is to develop principled methods for system security.
During my PhD I have designed information flow control techniques for securing IoT and WebAssembly applications. Currently, I am interested in developing language-based defenses against microarchitectural attacks.

I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Chalmers University of Technology under the supervision of Andrei Sabelfeld.

I am on the (academic) job market! I am interested in a hybrid/remote or Gothenburg-based research-related position.
